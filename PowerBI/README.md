# Power BI Projects

Interactive dashboards and business intelligence reports built with Microsoft Power BI.

## Projects

| Project | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| [Banking Risk Analysis](./Banking%20Risk%20Analysis/) | End-to-end Power BI dashboard analyzing 3,000 banking clients across risk profiles, loan portfolios, deposits, loyalty tiers, and investment advisor assignments |
| [Email Marketing Analysis](./Email_Marketing/) | Customer segmentation dashboard on 10,000 Indian customer records — analysing demographics, geography, gender, marital status, and living arrangements for targeted email campaigns |
| [Food Delivery Startup — Crisis Recovery](./Food_Delivery_Startup/) | Full data pipeline (Python EDA → Power BI) on 1.38M records from a food delivery startup — quantifying a 61% order decline and ₹19 Crore revenue loss caused by a viral incident and platform outage |
| [OTT Domain — Merger Analysis](./OTT_Domain/) | SQL + Power BI comparative analysis of two OTT platforms (LioCinema & Jotstar) across users, content, engagement, and revenue to support a telecom-streaming merger decision |
